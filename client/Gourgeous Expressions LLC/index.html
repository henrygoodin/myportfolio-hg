    Frequently Asked Questions

    Find answers to common questions about our services, policies, and what to expect at Gorgeous Expressions LLC.

    What services do you offer?

    We specialize in **brow waxing, body waxing, skincare treatments, and facials**. Our goal is to enhance your natural beauty with personalized, high-quality treatments.

    How do I prepare for a waxing appointment?

    For the best results, ensure your hair is at least **1/4 inch long** (about the length of a grain of rice). Avoid applying lotions or oils before your appointment, and gently exfoliate the area 24 hours beforehand.

    Does waxing hurt?

    Waxing may cause some discomfort, but we use professional techniques to minimize pain. The sensation varies based on personal tolerance, but with **regular waxing, the process becomes easier over time** as hair grows back finer.

    How should I care for my skin after a waxing session?

    Avoid **hot showers, saunas, and excessive sweating for 24 hours** after waxing. Keep the area moisturized with a gentle, fragrance-free lotion and exfoliate **2-3 times a week** to prevent ingrown hairs.
